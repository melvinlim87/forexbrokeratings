## Regulation & Safety: The Elephant in the Room

### VFSC Regulation Only

Let's address this upfront: Global Prime is **not regulated by any Tier-1 authority**. The primary entity is:

| Detail | Information |
|--------|-------------|
| **Regulator** | Vanuatu Financial Services Commission (VFSC) |
| **Entity** | Gleneagle Securities Pty Limited |
| **Company Number** | 40256 |
| **License Type** | Dealer in Securities |

The VFSC is considered a **Tier-3 regulatory jurisdiction**. While it does impose some basic requirements, it lacks:

- ❌ Investor compensation scheme (no ICF, no FSCS)
- ❌ Stringent capital requirements comparable to ASIC/FCA
- ❌ Regular independent audits
- ❌ Robust enforcement mechanisms

The Australian corporate presence (FMGP Trading Group Pty Ltd, ABN 74 146 086 017) operates the website but is **not an ASIC-regulated trading entity**. This is an important distinction — being headquartered in Australia is not the same as being regulated by ASIC.
