## Executive Summary

Orbex is a Cyprus-based forex and CFD broker established in 2010, regulated by CySEC and the FSC of Mauritius. Originally operating under the name "AFBFX," the company rebranded to Orbex in 2013 and has since grown to serve traders across Europe, the Middle East, and internationally.

The broker offers access to over 300 instruments through MetaTrader 4 and MetaTrader 5, with competitive spreads and multiple account types designed for different trading styles. Orbex has invested in educational content and market analysis, positioning itself as a broker that helps traders improve.

**Quick Verdict:** Orbex is a reliable, well-regulated broker that delivers solid trading conditions on MetaTrader platforms. It won't blow you away with innovation, but it provides a dependable trading environment with good educational resources. Best suited for traders in the EU and Middle East who value CySEC regulation.

**Bottom Line:** Orbex is a safe, regulated choice that does most things well without excelling in any area. If you're in the EU and want a reliable MT4/MT5 broker with solid educational support, Orbex is worth considering.
