## Regulation and Safety of Funds

OANDA's regulatory profile is among the strongest in the industry. The company operates through multiple regulated entities:

- **OANDA Corporation (US)**: Registered with the CFTC and a member of the NFA (ID: 0325821). This makes OANDA one of only a handful of brokers authorized to serve US-based retail forex traders — an incredibly demanding regulatory environment.
- **OANDA Europe Limited**: Authorized and regulated by the Financial Conduct Authority (FCA), registration number 542574. UK clients benefit from FSCS protection up to £85,000.
- **OANDA Australia Pty Ltd**: Regulated by the Australian Securities and Investments Commission (ASIC), AFSL #412981.
- **OANDA Asia Pacific Pte Ltd**: Licensed by the Monetary Authority of Singapore (MAS).
- **OANDA Canada**: Registered with the Investment Industry Regulatory Organization of Canada (IIROC).

Client funds are held in segregated accounts at top-tier banks, separate from OANDA's operational funds. This multi-jurisdictional regulatory coverage means that regardless of where you're based, your account is likely governed by a reputable financial authority.
