The **MetaTrader Supreme Edition** is Admirals' proprietary plugin that significantly enhances MT4 and MT5. It adds 60+ tools that transform the standard MetaTrader experience into a professional-grade trading terminal.

The Mini Terminal allows you to manage trades without switching windows. Features include:
- One-click order placement with automatic stop-loss and take-profit calculation

- Quick close-all and close-profit buttons
- Trade directly from the chart

#### Trade Terminal
The Trade Terminal provides a comprehensive view of all open positions across instruments:
- P&L breakdown by instrument and direction
- Bulk order management (close all, close losing, close winning)
- Risk exposure overview
- Account summary at a glance

The Tick Chart Trader provides tick-level analysis not available on standard MetaTrader:
- Real-time tick charts with customizable display
- Volume-at-price analysis
- Ideal for scalpers who need tick-level precision

See how other Admirals traders are positioned in real-time:
- Long/short ratio for each instrument

- Use as a contrarian indicator or confirmation tool

## Fees to Watch Out For

### Inactivity Fee
Admirals charges an inactivity fee of **$10 per month** after 24 months of no trading activity. While this is a reasonable threshold (2 years), traders who maintain dormant accounts should be aware of this charge.

### Currency Conversion Fee
When trading instruments denominated in a currency different from your account base currency, Admirals applies a currency conversion fee. This is standard across the industry but worth noting.

### Overnight/Swap Fees
Standard overnight financing charges apply to positions held past the daily rollover time. Admirals offers swap-free (Islamic) accounts for qualifying clients.
