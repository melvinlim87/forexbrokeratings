## Executive Summary

Windsor Brokers is one of the longest-established online forex brokers in the industry, having been founded in 1988 in Limassol, Cyprus. With over three decades of experience, the broker has built a reputation for stability, reliability, and regulatory compliance. Licensed by CySEC, FSC (Belize), and other regulators, Windsor Brokers serves clients across the Middle East, Africa, Europe, and Asia.

The broker offers access to forex, commodities, indices, shares, and bonds through MetaTrader 4 and MetaTrader 5, with competitive spreads and multiple account types catering to different trader profiles.

**Quick Verdict:** Windsor Brokers is a trustworthy, well-regulated broker with a long track record. Its longevity alone is a trust signal. However, its platform options and instrument range feel dated compared to newer, more innovative competitors. Best for traders who prioritize stability and regulation over cutting-edge features.

Windsor Brokers offers MetaTrader 4 and MetaTrader 5 on desktop, web, and mobile. The MT4 platform supports Expert Advisors (EAs) for automated trading, while MT5 offers additional timeframes and indicators.
