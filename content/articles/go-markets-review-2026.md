# GO Markets Review 2026: Australia's Quiet Achiever — Dual Tier-1 Regulated Broker

## Overview

GO Markets is an Australian-founded forex and CFD broker that has been operating since 2006. Founded by Chris Gore and headquartered in Melbourne, the company has grown from a small Australian brokerage into a multi-regulated global operation with entities in Australia (ASIC), Cyprus (CySEC), the United Kingdom (FCA), Mauritius (FSC), and Vanuatu (VFSC).

What makes GO Markets stand out is its dual tier-1 regulatory status. The broker holds both an Australian Financial Services License (AFSL 254963) from ASIC and FCA authorization (FRN 815452) from the UK's Financial Conduct Authority. This is a relatively rare combination that places GO Markets in an elite category of brokers with regulatory credentials that meet the gold standard in two of the world's most respected financial jurisdictions.

GO Markets focuses on providing competitive ECN pricing through the MetaTrader 4 and MetaTrader 5 platforms, complemented by additional tools like Autochartist (automated technical analysis) and Trading Central (professional market research). The broker also offers free VPS hosting for active algorithmic traders.

The company serves clients across Australia, the EU (via CySEC), the UK (via FCA), and internationally through its Mauritius and Vanuatu entities. The entity that services your account depends on your country of residence.

**Quick Verdict:** GO Markets is an excellent choice for Australian and UK-based traders seeking a well-regulated ECN broker with competitive pricing. Dual tier-1 regulation (ASIC + FCA), spreads from 0.0 pips, and solid MetaTrader support make it a quietly excellent option. Score: 78/100.
