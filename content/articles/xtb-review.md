## In-Depth Platform Analysis: xStation 5

### Desktop & Web Experience

xStation 5 is one of the most polished proprietary platforms in the industry. The interface is divided into customizable panels — chart, watchlist, order ticket, and open positions — that can be rearranged and resized. The dark and light themes are both well-executed, and the overall aesthetic is modern and professional.

Charting is where xStation 5 truly shines for a proprietary platform. You get **40+ technical indicators**, multiple chart types (candlestick, Heikin-Ashi, line, bar), and the ability to draw trendlines, Fibonacci retracements, and other annotations directly on the chart. While this doesn't match MT5's 80+ indicators, it's more than sufficient for the vast majority of retail traders.

### Advanced Features

xStation 5 includes several features that most proprietary platforms lack:

- **Trader's Calculator:** Instantly calculate margin requirements, pip values, and swap rates for any instrument before placing a trade.
- **Market Sentiment:** See the percentage of XTB traders who are long vs. short on each instrument — useful as a contrarian indicator.
- **Economic Calendar:** Integrated calendar with upcoming economic releases, filterable by impact level and country.
- **Heatmaps:** Visual representation of sector and market performance at a glance.
- **Stock Scanner:** Filter stocks by sector, performance, dividend yield, and other criteria.

These tools are integrated seamlessly into the platform, eliminating the need to juggle multiple external websites or tools.

### Mobile App Quality

The xStation 5 mobile app is one of the highest-rated trading apps available. It mirrors the web platform's core functionality, including charting with indicators, the economic calendar, and the trader's calculator. Order execution is fast, and the app handles complex order types (limit, stop, OTO) without issues.

One notable advantage: XTB's mobile app includes the full educational library — all 200+ lessons are accessible on mobile, making it easy to learn while commuting or away from your desk.
