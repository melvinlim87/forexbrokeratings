The CySEC license (#204/13) provides EU/EEA clients with MiFID II protections and ICF compensation up to €20,000. The FSA Seychelles entity (SD026) serves international clients.

**Key differentiator:** The NAGA Group AG is publicly listed on the Frankfurt Stock Exchange, requiring mandatory financial reporting — providing more transparency than typical private brokers. The company was founded in 2015 by Benjamin Bilski and has undergone corporate restructuring with The Core Fintech Group.
