### Critical Regulatory Warning

The majority of OctaFX's 15 million clients are served through the **SVG FSA entity**, which is important to understand:

**SVG FSA is NOT a forex regulator.** St. Vincent and the Grenadines does not regulate, license, or oversee forex brokerage operations. The SVG FSA merely registers international business companies. This provides virtually zero investor protection.

**EU Clients (CySEC):** Good protection — ICF coverage up to €20,000, negative balance protection, segregated funds, leverage capped at 30:1.

**International Clients (SVG):** Minimal protection — no compensation scheme, no regulatory oversight of trading operations, no leverage restrictions. Fund safety depends entirely on the broker's internal policies.
