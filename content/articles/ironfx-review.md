## Executive Summary

IronFX is a Cyprus-based forex and CFD broker founded in 2010 by Markos Kashiouris. With licenses from the FCA (UK), ASIC (Australia), CySEC (Cyprus), and FSCA (South Africa), IronFX offers broad regulatory coverage across four jurisdictions. The broker provides multiple account types including ECN accounts with raw spreads from 0.10 pips on EUR/USD — competitive by industry standards. However, past regulatory issues (a 2015 CySEC fine), mixed user reviews, and a standard account spread that trails the industry average temper its appeal. IronFX suits traders who prioritize ECN pricing and multi-jurisdiction regulation, but those seeking cutting-edge platform diversity or top-tier customer service may find better options elsewhere.

### What This Means For Your Money

**Segregated Funds:** Yes — client funds are held separately from company operating accounts in all regulated jurisdictions.

**Negative Balance Protection:** Yes — guaranteed on all entities, meaning you cannot lose more than your account balance.

**Compensation Schemes:**
- UK clients: Financial Services Compensation Scheme (FSCS) covers up to £85,000
- EU clients: Investor Compensation Fund (ICF) covers up to €20,000
- Australian clients: AFCA provides dispute resolution

### Regulatory Track Record

IronFX has not had a clean regulatory history. In 2015, CySEC fined the company €335,000 for operational failures related to client fund handling and reporting obligations. While the company has since restructured and improved compliance, this historical issue remains a consideration for risk-conscious traders. No major enforcement actions have occurred since 2017.

ECN spreads start from 0.10 pips on EUR/USD (below industry average of 0.27 pips). Standard floating spreads average 1.40 pips on EUR/USD (above the 1.11 industry average). Fixed spreads start from 1.8 pips.

### Does IronFX charge inactivity fees?

Yes. IronFX charges $50 per month after 6 months of inactivity. This is significantly higher than most competitors, which typically charge $10–$15 after 12 months.

### How long do IronFX withdrawals take?

Withdrawals typically take 2–5 business days depending on the payment method. IronFX does not charge withdrawal fees, but your bank or payment provider may apply charges.
