Regulation is the single most important factor when choosing a broker. Axi holds dual Tier 1 regulation — the gold standard in the industry — plus additional licenses for international coverage.

| Regulator | License Number | Region |
|-----------|---------------|--------|

| **FSA** (St. Vincent & The Grenadines) | Registered | International |

### What This Means For You

- **Negative balance protection** is provided across ASIC and FCA entities, meaning you can never lose more than your account balance.
- **Segregated client funds** are held at National Australia Bank (NAB) and other top-tier banks, separate from Axi's operating capital.
- **FSCS protection** (up to £85,000) applies to UK FCA-regulated accounts.
- **Regular audits** by both ASIC and FCA ensure ongoing compliance with the highest financial standards.
- **Professional indemnity insurance** provides an additional layer of protection.

**Important Note:** Non-AU/UK/EU clients may be onboarded to the SVG (St. Vincent) entity, which has lower regulatory protections. Always verify which entity you're opening an account with to understand the level of protection that applies to you.

Axi has been operating since 2007 with no major regulatory actions or scandals, which speaks volumes about its commitment to compliance and client safety.
