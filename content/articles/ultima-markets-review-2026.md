### Comprehensive Client Protections:

Beyond direct regulatory licenses, Ultima Markets reinforces client safety through several key mechanisms:

*   **Client Fund Segregation:** Across all its regulated entities, Ultima Markets adheres to the fundamental principle of segregating client funds. *   **Negative Balance Protection:** This crucial safeguard is standard across all Ultima Markets entities, ensuring that clients can never lose more than their deposited capital.
