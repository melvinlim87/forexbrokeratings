For beginners and intermediate traders seeking a well-regulated broker with a custom platform, ThinkMarkets is a solid choice.

## Verdict

ThinkMarkets is a solid all-rounder that's particularly well-suited to beginners (thanks to $0 minimum deposit) and intermediate traders (thanks to ThinkTrader and ThinkZero).
