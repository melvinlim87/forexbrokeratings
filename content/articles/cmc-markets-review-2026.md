## Regulation and Trust

CMC Markets' regulatory profile is exceptional. As a publicly listed company on the London Stock Exchange, CMC Markets is subject to additional financial transparency requirements beyond standard broker regulation.

### Regulatory Bodies
- **Financial Conduct Authority (FCA)** — Registration #173730. As a UK-based broker, CMC Markets is one of the primary FCA-regulated institutions. UK clients benefit from FSCS protection up to £85,000.
- **Australian Securities and Investments Commission (ASIC)** — AFSL #238054. CMC Markets Australia serves the Asia-Pacific region under ASIC's strict oversight.
- **Monetary Authority of Singapore (MAS)** — Licensed to serve Singapore-based traders.
- **BaFin (Germany)** — Registered for German operations post-Brexit.
- **IIROC (Canada)** — Registered with the Investment Industry Regulatory Organization of Canada.

Client funds are held in segregated trust accounts at major banks, separate from CMC Markets' corporate funds. As a publicly listed company, CMC Markets publishes audited financial results annually, providing an additional layer of transparency that most privately held brokers cannot match.

## The Next Generation Platform: A Deep Dive

CMC Markets' proprietary Next Generation platform is the centerpiece of their offering — and it's genuinely one of the best trading platforms available from any broker, proprietary or third-party.

### Charting Capabilities
The Next Generation platform features institutional-quality charting with:

- **115+ technical indicators** including advanced tools like Ichimoku Cloud, Elliott Wave patterns, and Gann analysis
- **70+ drawing tools** for technical analysis, including Fibonacci retracements, pitchforks, and harmonic pattern tools
- **12 chart types** including candlestick, Heikin Ashi, Renko, Point & Figure, and Kagi
- **Up to 250 indicators per chart** — no artificial limits
- **Multi-timeframe analysis** on a single screen
- **Chart-sync across devices** — your analysis carries over from web to mobile

The charting quality rivals dedicated platforms like TradingView, but with the advantage of being fully integrated with your brokerage account for one-click execution.

### Pattern Recognition
One of the Next Generation platform's standout features is its built-in automated pattern recognition. The platform scans your charts in real-time and identifies:
- Candlestick patterns (doji, hammer, engulfing, etc.)
- Chart patterns (head and shoulders, triangles, flags, wedges)
- Key support and resistance levels

This feature saves significant time for technical traders and can help identify trading opportunities you might otherwise miss.

### Client Sentiment Indicators
CMC Markets displays real-time client sentiment data showing the percentage of CMC clients who are long vs. short on each instrument. This contrarian indicator is valuable for gauging market positioning and identifying potential reversals.

### Customizable Workspace
The platform supports:
- **Drag-and-drop layout customization**
- **Multiple saved workspace configurations**
- **Split-screen chart views** (up to 8 simultaneous charts)
- **Price ladder (DOM)** for precise order entry

### Mobile App
The Next Generation mobile app is one of the highest-rated trading apps on both iOS (4.5/5) and Android (4.3/5). It includes nearly all desktop features including full charting, pattern recognition, and alerts.

## Guaranteed Stop-Loss Orders (GSLOs)

A standout risk management feature: CMC Markets offers Guaranteed Stop-Loss Orders (GSLOs) that protect against slippage and gapping — even during extreme market conditions like the Swiss Franc flash crash or major geopolitical events.

GSLOs come with a small premium (displayed at the time of order placement), but they provide absolute certainty about your maximum loss on a trade. This is a rare feature that very few brokers offer and is particularly valuable during volatile market conditions.
