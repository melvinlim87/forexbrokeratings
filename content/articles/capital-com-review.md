## Frequently Asked Questions

### Is Capital.com a legitimate broker?
Yes, Capital.com is regulated by the FCA (UK), CySEC (EU), ASIC (Australia), and SCB (Bahamas). It holds multiple top-tier licenses and maintains segregated client funds. The broker serves over 700,000 clients globally.

### What is the minimum deposit at Capital.com?
The minimum deposit is $20 (or equivalent in other currencies). This makes Capital.com one of the most accessible brokers for new traders who want to start with a small amount.

### Does Capital.com charge commissions?
No, Capital.com charges zero commissions on all instruments. The broker earns revenue from spreads, which start from 0.6 pips on EUR/USD. There are also no inactivity or withdrawal fees.

### Can I trade real stocks on Capital.com?
No, Capital.com offers stock CFDs (Contracts for Difference), not real stock ownership. This means you speculate on price movements without owning the underlying shares. Dividend adjustments are applied for long positions.

### What trading platforms does Capital.com offer?
Capital.com primarily uses its proprietary web and mobile platform, which features AI-powered insights and TradingView integration. MT4 is available for certain regions and account types. The proprietary platform is well-designed and suitable for most traders.

### Does Capital.com have a demo account?
Yes, Capital.com offers a free demo account with $1,000 in virtual funds. The demo account has no time limit and provides full access to all platform features, making it ideal for practice and strategy testing.
