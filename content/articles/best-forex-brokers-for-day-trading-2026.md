Day trading forex demands speed, precision, and a broker that won't eat into your margins with wide spreads or sluggish execution. Whether you're scalping EUR/USD during the London session or catching JPY moves at the Tokyo open, your broker choice can make or break your P&L.

We've tested dozens of brokers and analyzed real trading data to bring you the **5 best forex brokers for day trading in 2026** — ranked by execution quality, spreads, platform features, and regulation.

## What Makes a Great Day Trading Broker?

Before diving into our picks, here's what matters most for intraday traders:

- **Tight spreads** — Every pip counts when you're taking 10–50 trades per day. Look for raw spread accounts with spreads under 0.2 pips on EUR/USD.
- **Fast execution** — Slippage costs money. Top brokers execute orders in under 30ms on average.
- **Low commissions** — If your broker charges commission on top of spreads, make sure it's competitive ($3–$7 per round turn per standard lot).
- **Advanced platforms** — Charting, one-click trading, depth of market (DOM), and algorithmic trading support are essential.
- **Strong regulation** — Day traders need to trust their broker with fast deposits and withdrawals. Stick with Tier-1 regulated brokers.
- **No restrictions on scalping** — Some brokers impose minimum holding times. Avoid them.

With that framework in mind, here are our top 5 picks.
