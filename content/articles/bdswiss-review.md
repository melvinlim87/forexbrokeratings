### ⚠️ Critical Concerns

BDSwiss surrendered its CySEC and FCA licenses between 2021–2023, exiting the EU and UK markets entirely. The name "BDSwiss" is misleading — the broker has no connection to Switzerland and no Swiss regulation. The MISA Comoros license (#T2023244) provides minimal oversight: no capital adequacy requirements, no investor compensation scheme, and limited enforcement capabilities.

There is no Tier-1 regulation remaining. The UAE SCA license is the only reputable regulator, covering only UAE clients.
