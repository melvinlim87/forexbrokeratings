TrioMarkets is a Cyprus-based forex and CFD broker established in 2014, regulated by the Cyprus Securities and Exchange Commission (CySEC). The broker offers trading across forex, commodities, indices, shares, and cryptocurrencies through MetaTrader 4 and MetaTrader 5 platforms.

TrioMarkets positions itself as a straightforward, no-frills broker that focuses on providing competitive trading conditions without unnecessary complexity. With multiple account types and a low minimum deposit, it aims to serve traders across the experience spectrum.
