## Regulation & Trust

### Forex.com Regulation

Forex.com is operated by **GAIN Capital Group LLC**, a subsidiary of StoneX Group Inc. (NASDAQ: SNEX). It's registered with the **CFTC** and is a member of the **NFA** (NFA ID: 0339826). StoneX is a publicly traded financial services company with over $10 billion in client assets, adding a layer of corporate transparency that many private brokers lack.

Client funds are held in **segregated accounts** at top-tier US banks. Forex.com also carries excess SIPC insurance for additional protection.

### OANDA Regulation

OANDA is registered with the **CFTC** and is a member of the **NFA** (NFA ID: 0325821). Founded in 1996, it's one of the oldest online forex brokers still operating. OANDA Corporation is privately held but has built a strong reputation over nearly three decades.

Like Forex.com, OANDA maintains **segregated client funds** and adheres to strict US capital requirements.

**Winner: Tie** — Both are CFTC-regulated, NFA-registered, and maintain segregated accounts. You're in safe hands with either broker.

Both brokers offer a commission-based account with tighter spreads:

## Frequently Asked Questions

### Is Forex.com or OANDA better for beginners?

**OANDA is better for beginners.** The $0 minimum deposit means you can start small and learn without financial pressure. Its no-inactivity-fee policy also means you won't be penalized for taking breaks. The MarketPulse research portal is an excellent free learning resource.

### Are both Forex.com and OANDA safe?

Yes. Both are **CFTC-regulated and NFA-registered**, which are the highest standards for forex broker regulation globally. Client funds are held in segregated accounts at major US banks. Your money is as safe with either broker as it can be in the forex industry.

### Which broker has lower spreads: Forex.com or OANDA?

**Forex.com has tighter standard spreads** on average. On EUR/USD, Forex.com averages 1.0 pip vs OANDA's 1.2 pips on standard accounts. However, on commission-based (RAW/Core) accounts, both brokers offer identical spreads of approximately 0.2 pips on EUR/USD plus $5 per 100K.

### Can I trade crypto with Forex.com or OANDA?

Yes, but with differences. **Forex.com offers both CFD and spot crypto trading**, allowing you to own the underlying asset. **OANDA only offers crypto CFDs**, which are derivative contracts. If actual crypto ownership matters to you, Forex.com is the better choice.

### Does OANDA offer MT5?

**No.** As of 2026, OANDA only supports **MT4** and its proprietary OANDA Trade platform. If you specifically need MetaTrader 5, Forex.com is your best option among US-regulated brokers.

### Which broker is better for algorithmic trading?

**Forex.com has a slight edge** for algo traders due to its MT5 support, which offers faster backtesting, more timeframes, and the MQL5 programming language. Both brokers support MT4 with full EA (Expert Advisor) capability. OANDA's open API is also strong for custom algo development.
