### dealCancellation — easyMarkets' Unique Ace

This is the feature no other broker offers. **dealCancellation** lets you cancel a losing trade within up to 60 minutes of opening it, getting your original investment back minus a small fee. Think of it as a trading insurance policy.

- Available on the proprietary easyMarkets platform
- Fee depends on trade size and time remaining
- Up to 60-minute cancellation window
- Ideal for trading volatile news events

### Guaranteed Stop Loss

Another rare feature: easyMarkets guarantees your stop-loss price. Unlike most brokers where slippage can blow through your stop during fast markets, easyMarkets absorbs the difference. This is especially valuable during earnings season, central bank announcements, and flash crashes.

### Frozen Rate & Inside Viewer

- **Frozen Rate:** Lock in a specific price before executing a trade — gives you time to decide without the price moving against you.
- **Inside Viewer:** Real-time sentiment data showing how other easyMarkets traders are positioned. Useful as a contrarian indicator.
