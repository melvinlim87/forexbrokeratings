## Regulation & Safety: Is Skilling Legit?

### CySEC-Regulated With Offshore Option

Skilling operates through two main entities:

| Regulator | Tier | Entity | License Number |
|-----------|------|--------|----------------|
| **CySEC (Cyprus)** | Tier 1 | Skilling Ltd | #354/18 |
| **FSA (Seychelles)** | Tier 3 | Skilling (Seychelles) Limited | SD042 |
| **FCA (UK)** | Tier 1 | Skilling UK Limited | Pending/in process |

The **CySEC license (#354/18)** provides EU passporting, meaning Skilling can legally serve clients across the European Economic Area. This comes with standard ESMA protections:

- **Investor Compensation Fund (ICF):** Up to €20,000 per client
- **Negative balance protection:** Guaranteed for retail clients
- **Segregated client funds:** Held at Tier-1 banks
- **MiFID II compliance:** Full EU regulatory framework

The **FSA Seychelles entity (SD042)** serves international clients with higher leverage (up to 1:500) but lower regulatory protection. Traders should prefer the CySEC entity when possible.

## Platform Deep Dive

### Skilling Trader (Proprietary)

Skilling Trader is the broker's homegrown platform, available on web and mobile. The interface is clean and modern, clearly designed with beginners in mind. You get real-time charts with customizable timeframes, a reasonable selection of technical indicators, and one-click trading functionality.

The platform supports basic order types (market, limit, stop) and includes a handy economic calendar. It's not going to replace TradingView for advanced charting, but for the target audience — beginners and casual traders — it's more than adequate. The mobile version is well-optimized and performs smoothly even on older devices.

### Skilling cTrader

For more advanced traders, Skilling cTrader is a significant upgrade. cTrader is a professional-grade ECN platform that provides:

- **Level II pricing** — see the full depth of the order book
- **65+ chart indicators** — substantially more than the proprietary platform
- **cAlgo integration** — build and deploy algorithmic trading strategies
- **Advanced order types** — including OCO, trailing stops, and more
- **Copy trading integration** — connect with cTrader's social trading network

cTrader is widely regarded as the best alternative to MetaTrader, and having it as an option is a genuine competitive advantage for Skilling. The platform handles fast execution well, and the Level II data is invaluable for scalpers and day traders.

### MetaTrader 4 & 5

Skilling's MT4 and MT5 offerings are standard — you get the full desktop, web, and mobile versions with EA support, custom indicators, and all the features that have made MetaTrader the industry standard for two decades. Nothing special here, but nothing missing either.
