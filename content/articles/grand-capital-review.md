### What This Means For You

- **No Tier 1/2 Regulation:** Grand Capital does NOT hold licenses from FCA, ASIC, CySEC, or any equivalent regulator. This is the most critical factor.
- **SVG Registration ≠ Regulation:** The St. Vincent and the Grenadines FSA does NOT regulate forex brokers. It merely registers companies. There is no regulatory oversight of forex operations.
- **Mwali License (MISA):** A relatively new offshore regulator with limited international recognition and enforcement capabilities.
- **FinaCom Membership:** Provides independent dispute resolution and compensation up to $20,000 per case. This is better than nothing — but FinaCom is a self-funded industry body, not a government regulator. Its protections are substantially weaker than ICF (CySEC) or FSCS (FCA).
- **No Investor Compensation Fund:** No government-backed compensation scheme.
- **Segregated Funds:** Claimed by the broker, but not verified by regulatory audits.
