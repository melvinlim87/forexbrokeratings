## Executive Summary

Tradeview Markets is a Cayman Islands-based ECN forex broker founded in 2004, regulated by the Cayman Islands Monetary Authority (CIMA). The broker has built a strong reputation among professional and algorithmic traders for its ultra-competitive ECN pricing, multiple platform options (including cTrader), and commitment to transparent, no-dealing-desk execution.

With over 150 instruments and access to MetaTrader 4, MetaTrader 5, and cTrader, Tradeview Markets caters to serious traders who demand institutional-grade trading conditions with tight spreads and fast execution.

**Quick Verdict:** Tradeview Markets is an excellent choice for experienced traders and algorithmic traders who want competitive ECN pricing with the flexibility of cTrader or MetaTrader. The CIMA regulation is decent but not top-tier, and the limited instrument range may not suit all traders.

## Frequently Asked Questions

### Is Tradeview Markets a regulated broker?
Yes, Tradeview Markets is regulated by the Cayman Islands Monetary Authority (CIMA) under License 585163. While CIMA is a recognized offshore regulator, it is not considered as stringent as ASIC, FCA, or CySEC.

### What platforms does Tradeview Markets offer?
Tradeview Markets offers MetaTrader 4, MetaTrader 5, cTrader, and Currenex (FIX API). The cTrader platform is particularly popular for its advanced charting and algorithmic trading capabilities.

### What is the minimum deposit at Tradeview Markets?
The minimum deposit is $100 for the ILQ, XLev, and cTrader accounts. The Currenex account requires $5,000.

### What is cTrader?
cTrader is an advanced trading platform known for its superior charting, Level 2 pricing, and cAlgo feature for algorithmic trading using C#. It also includes cTrader Copy for social/copy trading.

### Does Tradeview Markets charge inactivity fees?
No, Tradeview Markets does not charge inactivity fees, which is a positive feature compared to many competitors.

### What is the maximum leverage at Tradeview Markets?
Maximum leverage is up to 1:400 on most accounts. The Currenex institutional account is limited to 1:100.

## Final Verdict

Tradeview Markets is a specialist ECN broker that punches above its weight in execution quality and platform choice. The availability of cTrader with copy trading, MT4/MT5, and Currenex with FIX API access makes it one of the most platform-diverse brokers in the industry. **Best For:**
- Algorithmic traders needing cTrader/cAlgo or FIX API
- Scalpers seeking ultra-tight ECN spreads
- Traders who want platform choice (MT4, MT5, cTrader)
- Professional traders needing Currenex institutional access

**Not Ideal For:**
- Traders who prioritize top-tier regulation (FCA, ASIC)
- Those seeking a wide range of instruments
- Beginners needing educational resources

**Bottom Line:** Tradeview Markets is a hidden gem for serious traders. If you value ECN execution quality, platform choice, and competitive pricing over regulation pedigree, this broker deserves a spot on your shortlist.
