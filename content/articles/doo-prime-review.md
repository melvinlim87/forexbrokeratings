### What This Means For You

- **VFSC (700238):** The primary offshore entity for many global clients. Vanuatu provides a moderate regulatory framework but no investor compensation scheme. Limited regulatory recourse in disputes.
- **FSA Seychelles (SD090):** Higher leverage available (up to 1:500). Limited regulatory oversight compared to Tier-1 jurisdictions.
- **FSC Mauritius (GB19024710):** A Tier-3 jurisdiction with a growing regulatory framework. Some investor protections, but below Tier-1 standards.
- **LFSA Labuan (MB/19/0031):** Malaysian offshore jurisdiction with moderate regulatory framework. Primarily serves Southeast Asian clients.

### Critical Point

**Doo Prime has no Tier-1 regulatory license.** No FCA (UK), ASIC (Australia), or CySEC (EU/EEA) authorization exists. This means no FSCS protection, no ICF coverage, and no regulatory body with strong enforcement power overseeing client funds. While the multi-entity structure provides jurisdictional choice, none of these jurisdictions match the investor protection standards of Tier-1 regulators.
