## Why MT5 Is the Future of Retail Forex Trading

### Key Advantages of MT5 Over MT4

MetaTrader 5 isn't just an upgrade — it's a fundamentally different trading platform with capabilities that MT4 simply cannot match:

- **21 timeframes** vs MT4's 9 — enabling more granular multi-timeframe analysis
- **38 built-in technical indicators** vs MT4's 30
- **Depth of Market (DOM)** — view the full order book in real-time
- **Economic calendar** integrated directly into the platform
- **6 pending order types** vs MT4's 4 (including buy stop limit and sell stop limit)
- **Netting and hedging position systems** — choose how positions are managed
- **Multi-threaded strategy tester** — backtest EAs faster with multi-core support
- **MQL5 programming language** — more powerful than MQL4 with OOP support
- **12+ months of historical data** accessible for backtesting

### Why Some Traders Still Resist MT5

The main reason some traders cling to MT4 is **EA compatibility**. MQL4 Expert Advisors don't work on MT5 — they need to be rewritten in MQL5. However, by 2026, the vast majority of popular EAs have been ported to MQL5, and the MQL5 marketplace is significantly larger than the MQL4 equivalent. If you're starting fresh, there's no reason to choose MT4 over MT5.
