### What This Means For You

- **St. Lucia IBC:** This is a company registration, not a forex-specific regulatory license. St. Lucia does not have a comprehensive regulatory framework for forex trading.
- **SVG Registration:** St. Vincent and the Grenadines does NOT regulate forex brokers — this registration merely creates a legal entity.
- **No Investor Compensation:** There is no formal deposit protection scheme or investor compensation fund.
- **No Tier 1/2 Regulation:** NordFX does NOT hold licenses from FCA, ASIC, CySEC, or any other major financial regulator.
- **Negative Balance Protection:** Offered voluntarily by the broker (not mandated by regulation)
- **Segregated Funds:** Claimed by the broker, but not independently verified by a regulatory body
