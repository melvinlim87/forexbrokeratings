# Infinox Review 2026: FCA-Regulated STP/ECN Broker — Premium Execution, Premium Price


---

## Overview

Infinox is a London-headquartered forex and CFD broker founded in 2009 by CEO Robert Berkeley. With over 16 years of operating history, Infinox has built a reputation as a premium, execution-focused broker that prioritizes trading quality over low entry barriers.

What distinguishes Infinox from the crowded forex broker market is its regulatory pedigree. Infinox Capital Ltd holds authorization from the UK's Financial Conduct Authority (FCA Register Number 501025) — widely considered the gold standard of financial regulation globally. FCA regulation comes with stringent requirements for client money segregation, capital adequacy, and investor protection through the Financial Services Compensation Scheme (FSCS) covering up to £85,000.

Infinox operates a dual-entity model: the FCA-regulated UK entity serves British and European clients under strict oversight, while a Bahamas-regulated entity (Securities Commission of The Bahamas, License SIA-F188) serves international clients with access to higher leverage.

The broker offers 300+ trading instruments across forex, commodities, indices, stocks, and cryptocurrencies through a pure STP/ECN execution model with no dealing desk intervention. This means Infinox passes trades directly to liquidity providers without taking the opposite side of client trades — eliminating a key conflict of interest present at market-maker brokers.

In this comprehensive 2026 review, we examine Infinox's regulation, trading conditions, platforms, fees, and whether the premium pricing ($1,000 minimum deposit) is justified.

**Quick Verdict:** Infinox is a serious broker for serious traders. FCA regulation, pure STP/ECN execution, and 16+ years of track record make it a trustworthy choice. However, the $1,000 minimum deposit, $7 round-turn ECN commission, and limited instrument range position it firmly in the mid-to-premium segment. Best for intermediate-to-professional traders who prioritize regulatory safety and execution quality over rock-bottom pricing.

### FCA Regulation — The Gold Standard

Infinox's FCA authorization (Register Number 501025) is significant for several reasons:

- **Client money rules** — Client funds must be held in segregated accounts per the FCA's Client Assets Sourcebook (CASS)
- **Capital requirements** — Infinox must maintain minimum capital reserves at all times
- **FSCS protection** — UK clients are covered by the Financial Services Compensation Scheme up to £85,000
- **Senior management oversight** — FCA holds named individuals accountable for compliance
- **Regular reporting** — Periodic regulatory filings and audits
- **Conduct rules** — Strict requirements around fair treatment of clients

### Dual Entity Model

| Entity | Regulator | License | Compensation | Max Leverage (Retail) |
|--------|-----------|---------|-------------|----------------------|
| Infinox Capital Ltd (UK) | FCA | 501025 | FSCS up to £85,000 | 1:30 |
| Infinox Capital Ltd (Bahamas) | SCB | SIA-F188 | Not guaranteed | 1:500 |

UK and EU clients are onboarded under the FCA entity, benefiting from the highest level of regulatory protection. International clients outside the UK/EU are onboarded under the Bahamas entity, which offers higher leverage but significantly fewer investor protections.

## Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

### Is Infinox regulated?
Yes. Infinox Capital Ltd is authorized and regulated by the UK Financial Conduct Authority (FCA Register Number 501025). International clients are served through Infinox Capital Ltd (Bahamas), regulated by the Securities Commission of The Bahamas (SCB License SIA-F188).

### What FSCS protection does Infinox offer?
UK clients of the FCA-regulated entity are covered by the Financial Services Compensation Scheme (FSCS) up to £85,000 per person in the event of broker insolvency.

### What is the minimum deposit for Infinox?
Both the Standard and ECN accounts require a $1,000 minimum deposit. The Pro account requires $25,000, and the VIP account requires $100,000+.

### What is Infinox's EUR/USD spread?
On the Standard account, EUR/USD spreads start from 0.8 pips. On the ECN account, spreads start from 0.2 pips with a $7 round-turn commission per standard lot.

### What is Infinox's ECN commission?
Infinox charges $7 round turn per standard lot on ECN accounts. This is slightly above the industry average of $3-6 but is competitive for FCA-regulated brokers.

### What platforms does Infinox offer?
Infinox supports MetaTrader 4 (MT4), MetaTrader 5 (MT5), its proprietary IX Trade web platform, and a mobile app for iOS and Android.

### Does Infinox allow scalping and EA trading?
Yes. Infinox's pure STP/ECN model fully supports scalping, hedging, and Expert Advisor (EA) trading. VPS hosting is compatible.

### Is Infinox available in the US?
No. Infinox does not accept clients from the United States and several other restricted jurisdictions.
