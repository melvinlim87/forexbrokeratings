Spreadex is the only broker in the world that offers both sports spread betting and financial spread betting from a single account. Here's how sports spread betting works:

### How It Works
- Instead of fixed odds, you bet on the "spread" of an outcome
- Example: Spreadex sets the total goals spread for a football match at 2.8–3.1
- If you think there will be more goals, you "buy" at 3.1 for £10 per point
- If there are 5 goals, you win (5 - 3.1) × £10 = £19
- If there are 2 goals, you lose (3.1 - 2) × £10 = £11
