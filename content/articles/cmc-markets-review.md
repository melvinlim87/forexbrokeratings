- **Publicly listed** — CMC Markets is listed on the London Stock Exchange (LSE: CMCX), providing additional transparency through mandatory financial reporting.

## The Next Generation Platform — CMC's Crown Jewel

The **Next Generation** platform is CMC Markets' proprietary web-based trading platform, and it's arguably the most feature-rich retail trading platform available in 2026.

#### Pattern Recognition
The built-in pattern recognition engine automatically identifies **120+ chart patterns** in real-time, including:
- Head and shoulders (and inverse)
- Double/triple tops and bottoms
- Triangles (ascending, descending, symmetrical)
- Flags, pennants, and wedges

This is one of the most advanced automated pattern recognition systems available on any retail trading platform.

- **Price alerts** — Set alerts for any price level and receive notifications via email, SMS, or push
- **News feed** — Integrated Reuters news stream
- **Sentiment data** — See how other CMC Markets traders are positioned

#### Market Finder
The Market Finder tool helps you identify trading opportunities by scanning across all 12,000+ instruments for:
- Unusual volume activity
- Breaking support/resistance levels

- High-volatility opportunities
