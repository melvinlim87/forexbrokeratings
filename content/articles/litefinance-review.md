## Quick Verdict

LiteFinance (formerly LiteForex) has operated since 2005 — over 20 years in the forex market. However, the broker holds no meaningful regulatory license. Its CySEC license was withdrawn when European operations ceased, leaving only SVG and Marshall Islands registrations — neither of which actually regulate forex brokers. With 600+ instruments, 1:1000 leverage, and copy trading features, the trading conditions are competitive, but the lack of regulation is a serious concern. We rate LiteFinance **58/100**.

**Best For:** Experienced traders who understand and accept offshore broker risks, traders wanting extreme leverage, copy trading enthusiasts in emerging markets.
